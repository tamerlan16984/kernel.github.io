About Our Hour of Code

As team 64, we conducted “Hour of Code” event with the residents of the Children's shelter-reintegration center operating under the Azerbaijan Children’s Union at the Globus Center, Jafar Jabbarli st. Our goal was to introduce students to basic coding concepts. The sessions started with giving brief introduction to ADA University and the School of IT and Engineering, then we explained basic algorithms and coding concepts, using the code.org platform as a guide. Throught the session, our team circulated the room, offering additional support to students who needed it. By the end of the event, all the students successfully completed the “Minecraft Adventurer” project and received certificates.
